circfull
============

About
-----------

circfull is a full-length circRNA analysis pipeline for circFL-seq data. It supports
reference-guided and de novo workflows to detect, quantify, and annotate circRNA
isoforms, and includes modules for strand identification, gene expression evaluation,
and variant calling.
