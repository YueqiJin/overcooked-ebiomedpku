## About

Deep-TEIRI is a pipeline to identify TE-initiated transcripts by integrating DNA sequence and RNA-seq coverage information with a deep-learning model.

The upstream repository includes:

- Pipeline scripts: https://github.com/yunzhang1998/Deep-TEIRI/tree/main/script
- Trained model files: https://github.com/yunzhang1998/Deep-TEIRI/tree/main/Model
