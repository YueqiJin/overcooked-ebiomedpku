## About

TEIRI is a pipeline to identify TE-initiated RNAs by integrating short-read RNA-seq, long-read RNA-seq, CAGE, and RAMPAGE datasets.

The upstream repository includes:

- Pipeline scripts: https://github.com/yunzhang1998/TEIRI/tree/main/scripts
- TE-initiated RNA annotation files: https://github.com/yunzhang1998/TEIRI/tree/main/annotation
