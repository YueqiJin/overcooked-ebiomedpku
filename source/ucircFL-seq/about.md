# About ucircFL-seq & ucircfull

![ucircFL-seq](../images/ucircFL-logo.png)

## About
ucircFL-seq is an amplification optimized and unique molecular identifier guided full-length circRNA sequencing method to profile high accuracy ordinary and fusion circRNA at the isoform level.
